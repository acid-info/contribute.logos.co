Welcome to Logos! We're thrilled to have you join us in shaping the decentralised web. Whether you're a developer, writer, or bring other valuable skills, there are plenty of ways to get involved and make an impact.

To get started, you can submit your ideas or projects through the [proposal form](/proposals).

You can also explore tailored guides on how to contribute based on your skills:

- [For Developers](/resources/developers)
- [For Writers](/resources/writers)
- [For Event Organisers](/resources/event-organisers)
- [For Translators](/resources/translators)
- [For Designers](/resources/designers)
