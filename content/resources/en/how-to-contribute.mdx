Welcome to Logos! We're thrilled to have you join us in shaping the decentralised web. Whether you're a developer, writer, designer, or bring other valuable skills, there are plenty of ways to get involved and make an impact.

To get started, create a post with your proposal in the [Logos forum](https://forum.logos.co/).

You can also explore tailored guides on how to contribute based on your skills:

- [To contribute to local grassroots initiatives](/resources/activists)
- [For developers](/resources/developers)
- [For writers](/resources/writers)
- [For event organisers](/resources/event-organisers)
- [For translators](/resources/translators)
- [For designers](/resources/designers)
